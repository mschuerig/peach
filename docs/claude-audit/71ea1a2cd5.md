### Story 9.2: Rethink Profile Display and Progress Tracking

**As a** musician using Peach,
**I want** to see my pitch discrimination progress as a threshold convergence chart over time,
**So that** I can see meaningful evidence that training is working, reflecting that pitch discrimination is one unified skill rather than many per-note skills.

**Rationale:** Story 9.1 established that pitch discrimination is one unified skill (Hawkey et al. 2004, Irvine et al. 2000). The current per-note confidence band over a piano keyboard is misleading ‚Äî per-note differences are mostly data collection artifacts, not real perceptual differences. A time-series chart showing threshold convergence is more honest, more motivating, and more scientifically sound.
