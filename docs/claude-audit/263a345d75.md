## Commit message

Fix CSV export/import round-trip: drop fractional seconds entirely

Fractional seconds caused duplicate detection failures because
Date has sub-millisecond precision that the ISO8601 formatter
rounds unpredictably at millisecond boundaries. Since training
comparisons are seconds apart, whole-second timestamps are
sufficient. Export reverts to .iso8601 (no fractional seconds),
and DuplicateKey truncates to whole seconds via Int64 conversion.

Co-Authored-By: Claude Opus 4.6 <noreply@anthropic.com>
